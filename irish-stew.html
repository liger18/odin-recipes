Irish Stew

Delious Irish stew

    Ingredients
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • onions
  • beef
  • beef stock
  • celery
  • thyme
  • corriander
  • butter(Kerry Gold)

    Steps
  1. Chop up onions and fry in butter until onions soften
  2. Dice beef and coat in four , add to pot, cooked till golden brown
  3. Add beef broth and bring to a boil
  4. Chop rest of vegtables and add
  5. Cover pot and cook on low heat for a futher 2hrs
  6. Garnish with corriander and Enjoy!!
